Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Stands out from the variant popularized by the Gracie family due to its incorporation of impactful techniques such as punches, kicks, elbows, and knees, along with takedowns, throws, and joint locks. Moreover, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu encompasses the handling of various weapons like sticks, knives, swords, and more, offering a comprehensive training experience.

Self-defense

Training incorporates techniques that enhance readiness, physical power, build resilience, and promote focus and stability.

Judo

Matches unfold on a designated platform called the tatami, with the aim of scoring points through either throwing the opponent to the ground or compelling them to touch their shoulders to the mat. The objective in Judo is to achieve victory by either executing a throw that brings the opponent down or by immobilizing them on the ground for a duration of thirty seconds.

Mission statement

The aim is to teach students the fundamental techniques of Brazilian Jiu Jitsu. Students will start a 16 week Beginners program. Here students will be taught the basic techniques and strategies of grappling in a friendly and relaxed environment. Jiu-Jitsu has become one of the most popular martial arts in Ireland due to its flexibility for all ages.
